SN-1	Real-Time Weather	I want to see real-time temperature, humidity, and atmospheric pressure readings.
SN-2	Historical Tracking	I want to track historical weather data over days and weeks.
SN-3	Weather Alerts		I want to receive alerts when weather conditions reach certain levels I care about.
SN-4	Multi-Device Access	I want to access the weather station data from my smartphone, tablet, or computer.
SN-5	Data Comparison		I want to compare current weather readings with previous days or similar conditions.
SN-6	Reliability		I want the system to be reliable and operate continuously without constant supervision.
SN-7	Usability		I want the system to be easy to understand and interpret, even with advanced data.
